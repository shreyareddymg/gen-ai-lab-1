# Part 2 – Daily Routine Motivation Story

This part of **Generative AI Lab–1** focuses on creating a **short motivational video** using Generative AI tools.  
The video highlights the importance of **small, consistent efforts in daily life**.

---

## Objective

- Use Generative AI to convert text into a motivational video
- Practice **prompt structuring for storytelling**
- Generate short-form content suitable for social media

---

## Story Concept

The story emphasizes:
- Consistency over intensity
- Small daily efforts
- Personal growth without pressure

---

## Workflow

1. Story script written using ChatGPT
2. Video generated using Fliki.ai
3. AI voice narration and background music applied
4. Screenshots captured for documentation

---

## Tools Used

- ChatGPT – Story script generation
- Fliki.ai – Text-to-video generation
- Google Drive – Video hosting

---

## Output

- Script: `prompts/story_script.txt`
- Video screenshots: `outputs/video_frames/`
- Full video link provided in references

---

## Learning Outcome

- Understood short-form AI storytelling
- Learned prompt-based narration control
- Gained experience in AI-assisted video generation
